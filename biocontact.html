Bio/Contact



Cim Thomakos is a visual artist. For 28 years she has been teaching and developing curriculum for secondary level and IB Visual Arts programs at ACS International Schools in Athens, Greece. As a National Writing Project Teacher Consultant, she conducts research, present workshops, and has instructed teacher training programs (K-college) in the uses of Writing Across the Curriculum. She is a published writer and illustrator, and has had solo exhibitions in the United States and Greece.

Boxes and Other Possibilities captured the attention of Pulitzer Prize winning journalist Laura Sessions Stepp. She wrote about it in the article "Giving Students' Imaginations Room to Soar," Washington Post, February, 2001.
