Workshops


Boxes is about possibilities. It can be adapted to a variety of programs. It is compatible with history, literature, science, and math. It can be developed into multi-level training and professional development programs in education, business, and the corporate world. It can encourage cooperation among professionals in the humanities and sciences. It is an uplifting, motivating, and inspiring experience.

Cim Thomakos offers a variety of Boxes Workshops for students and adults. Workshops are designed to stimulate interest, stretch thinking, and encourage collaboration. Participants manipulate metaphors, generate multiple solutions to problems, and build their own ways through conceptual and technical challenges to realize unpredictable outcomes. Boxes Workshops engage participants in an authentic creative process.
